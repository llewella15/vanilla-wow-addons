MyAcePercentage

Author:   instant (instant0@gmail.com
Version:  b.1.7.0
Release:  2005-09-27
Website:  http://www.wowace.com [Might as well go there.. :-) ]
_____________________________________________________________________________________


This is a simple addon to display in the tooltip of spells the current
and/or total mana the spell will cost, in percentage. 

Example tooltip:

Blizzard North
180 Mana (t:18.1%)(c:25.5%) 
This will rain down lots of icy stuff on
thine enemies to smithe them, in thy glory

_____________________________________________________________________________________

	FEATURES
_____________________________________________________________________________________

- Lets you see total and/or current mana in % for spell tooltips
- Does NOT use OnUpdate as the Non-ACED version of MyPercentage did
_____________________________________________________________________________________

	USAGE
_____________________________________________________________________________________

Command: /myperc

/myperc ?		: Displays extra information about mypercentage
/myperc standby	: Toggles the addon's standby mode. Standby makes the addon active or
			      inactive, though it is still loaded.
/myperc total	: toggles displaying mana cost in % of total mana
/myperc current	: toggles displaying mana cost in % of current mana
/myperc colour  : toggles displaying colour OR t:/c:
